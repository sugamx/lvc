Scholarship Terms & Conditions

Eligibility

Students must maintain a minimum average percentage as specified in the scholarship criteria. Academic performance will be evaluated based on both 10th and 12th grade results.

Scholarship Benefits

- 50% scholarship for students with average ≥90%
- 25% scholarship for students with average ≥80% and <90%
- Scholarship amount will be deducted from the total course fee

Important Notes

- All submitted documents must be authentic and verifiable
- The scholarship decision is final and binding
- The institute reserves the right to modify terms and conditions
